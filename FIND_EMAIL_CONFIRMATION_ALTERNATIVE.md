# 🔍 Alternative Ways to Find Email Confirmation Setting

## 📍 You've Scrolled Down But Still Can't Find It

The setting might be in a different location or called something different.

---

## 🔍 Try These Options:

### Option 1: Scroll to VERY TOP of Emails Page

1. **Scroll to the VERY TOP** of the Emails page
2. **Look for** settings like:
   - "Enable email confirmations"
   - "Email confirmation required"
   - "Require email verification"
   - "Email verification for new users"

**It might be at the top, above "Secure email change"!**

---

### Option 2: Check URL Configuration Page Again

Some Supabase versions have it there:

1. Click **"URL Configuration"** in left sidebar
2. **Scroll ALL the way down** on that page
3. Look for **"Email Confirmation"** section
4. Find **"Enable email confirmations"** toggle

---

### Option 3: Check "Sign In / Providers" Page

1. Click **"Sign In / Providers"** in left sidebar
2. Look for email-related settings
3. Check for confirmation options

---

### Option 4: It Might Be Called Something Else

Look for these labels:
- ✅ "Enable email confirmations"
- ✅ "Email confirmation required"
- ✅ "Require email verification"
- ✅ "Email verification for new users"
- ✅ "Confirm email on signup"
- ✅ "Email verification toggle"

---

### Option 5: Check Top of Emails Page

1. **Scroll to the VERY TOP** of Emails page
2. **Before "Secure email change"** section
3. Look for any toggle related to email confirmation
