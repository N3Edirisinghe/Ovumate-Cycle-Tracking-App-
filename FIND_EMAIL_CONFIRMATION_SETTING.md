# 🔍 Where to Find "Enable email confirmations" Setting

## 📍 You're Currently On:
**Authentication → Users** page

## ✅ Where to Go Instead:

### Option 1: URL Configuration (Most Likely Here)

1. **Left sidebar** → Click **"URL Configuration"**
2. Scroll down to find **"Email Confirmation"** section
3. Look for **"Enable email confirmations"** toggle
4. Turn it **OFF** ⚪

---

### Option 2: Emails Section

1. **Left sidebar** → Click **"Emails"**
2. Look for **"Email Confirmation"** or **"Enable email confirmations"** toggle
3. Turn it **OFF** ⚪

---

### Option 3: Settings (If Available)

1. **Left sidebar** → Look for **"Settings"** option
2. If you see it, click it
3. Scroll to find **"Email Confirmation"** section
4. Turn **"Enable email confirmations"** **OFF** ⚪

---

## 🎯 Quick Steps:

1. **Look at the LEFT SIDEBAR** (where you see "Users", "Policies", etc.)
