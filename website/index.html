Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about OvuMate

How does OvuMate track my cycle?

OvuMate uses advanced algorithms to track your menstrual cycle based on the data you input, including period dates, symptoms, and lifestyle factors. The app learns your patterns over time to provide accurate predictions.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, your privacy is our top priority. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use OvuMate without an internet connection?

Yes, OvuMate works offline for basic tracking features. However, some features like cloud sync and partner sharing require an internet connection.

How accurate are the predictions?

Prediction accuracy improves over time as the app learns your cycle patterns. For best results, track your cycle consistently for at least 3 months.

Is OvuMate free to use?

OvuMate offers a free version with essential features. Premium features are available through a subscription plan.
